Who We Are

About SOELSEARCH

SOELSEARCH is a student-led research initiative at the School of Excellence in Law (SOEL), guided by the Internal Quality Assurance Cell (IQAC) of The Tamil Nadu Dr. Ambedkar Law University. Our aim is to build a vibrant, inclusive, and sustainable research ecosystem that equips students with the skills and mentorship necessary for rigorous legal inquiry and impactful scholarship. We support research training, academic publishing, collaborative projects, and professional development — all grounded in the principles of academic integrity and social justice.

Vision

To cultivate an enduring research culture at SOEL that fosters academic growth, legal innovation, and collaborative inquiry across diverse legal disciplines.

Objectives

  • Encourage and mentor students in rigorous legal research and writing.
  • Provide institutional support for publishing, competitions, and collaborations.
  • Connect students with faculty, alumni, and academic partners for holistic development.
  • Facilitate training in research methodologies, citation practices, and e-resources.

What We Do

Our Activities

SOELSEARCH runs a structured program of workshops, competitions, collaborations, and publications designed to develop research skills and showcase student work.

Research Workshops and Training

Hands-on workshops on doctrinal and empirical methods, citation systems (Bluebook, OSCOLA, APA), academic writing, and legal databases such as SCC Online and HeinOnline. Certificate courses and practical training help students apply research methods confidently.

Competitions and Monthly Challenges

Inter- and intra-college contests — research paper competitions, policy critiques, legislative drafting, and judgment deconstruction. Monthly thematic challenges highlight short submissions on pressing issues (Technology and Law, Gender Justice, Labour Rights).

Publications and Mentoring

Student articles, case notes, and an Annual Research Digest. Peer review and faculty mentoring support external submissions, and a Research Bulletin lists curated calls for papers and opportunities.

Other Initiatives

  • Collaborations with academic partners, conferences, and guest lectures.
  • Legal writing clinics and research mentoring programs.
  • Annual SOELSEARCH Research Conclave and curated research bulletin.

Structure and Governance

SOELSEARCH operates under IQAC guidance and is supported by a Faculty Advisory Board and a Student Core Committee. Appointments and selection are merit-based, guided by transparent criteria emphasizing research experience and academic interest.
